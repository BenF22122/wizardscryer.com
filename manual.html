How to use Wizard of the Scrying Tower

Wizard of the Scrying Tower is a free diagnostic tool that helps you identify and resolve issues with your PC and network.

After installation, run the application and choose the spell you want to cast. Each spell reveals useful information about your device or the network you’re connected to.
