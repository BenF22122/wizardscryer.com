About Wizard of the Scrying Tower

Our application was born out of a simple philosophy: powerful diagnostics shouldn't have to look boring, and simple interfaces shouldn't have to be weak.

Whether you choose the Apprentice or Master edition, you are utilizing the exact same core engine. This engine scans local hardware configurations, detects memory leaks, analyzes network packet drops, maps local routing tables, and sniffs out DNS bottlenecks in real-time. The only difference is how the magic is revealed to you.

Help & Frequently Asked Questions

Does the Apprentice version miss any scanning features?

No. Both versions perform the exact same deep-system and network sweeps. The Apprentice version simply hides complex raw telemetry behind friendly UI indicators.

Why isn't the Wizard signed?

Wizard of the Scrying Tower is a new project, and code‑signing certificates require a verified publisher identity and a reputation history. As the software gains real‑world usage, a trusted digital signature will be implemented to remove SmartScreen warnings and provide additional security assurance.

What operating systems are supported?

Currently, Wizard of the Scrying Tower supports Windows 10 & 11.
